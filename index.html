Raghavendra Deeduvanu

Product Manager

Project Manager with varied experience in the IT industry building and developing software products.

I lead all product management efforts at Think Tankers Innovative Solutions where I have successfully managed all phases of technology projects, built business critical systems such as cloud-based ERP solutions, and helped enhance customer experience. These products have helped end users understand the quality of their data and make better business decisions.

I am a cross-functional collaborator who enjoys working closely with engineering teams and with end users. I have worked with clients in the logistics and supply chain, health and wellness, and education sectors.

In the roles held by me I’ve been recognized as a:

  • Strong communicator with robust stakeholder management skills acquired through direct interaction with customers, technology teams, and leadership to set measurable and achievable targets.
  • Business Strategist who can leverage technologies and project management principles to plan high profile projects.
  • Risk Champion who proactively identifies risks and dependencies impacting a project’s timely delivery and outline pre-emptive and early mitigating actions.
  • Skilled Mentor to human assets to coach and lead them towards a collaborative approach to project completion.
